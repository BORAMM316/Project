Transfer Points

The maximum amount for cashing out is 6,000 points.

To register/amend account information go to My Information > Account Information.

To cash out points, the name authentication is needed.

The minimum point for cashing out is 5,000 points, and the points can be cashed out   in 1,000 points.

3.3% business income tax will be deducted.

  If it is less than 1,000 won the amount will be refunded on the 10th of the next month.

Cashing out is limited to once a week, and you can findthe status on the Transfer   Points tab.

The weekly deadline for cashing out is Thursday 2 pm and the cashing out requests   completed before the deadline will be paid on the following Friday at 2 pm (The   requests made after Thursday 2 pm will be paid next Friday).

Cashing out request is unavailable during 14:01 to 15:00 on Thursdays due to system   maintenance.

Cashing out process : name authentication > cashing out request > waiting for   payment > payment approval > payment completed

The new request is available when there is no payment in waiting.
